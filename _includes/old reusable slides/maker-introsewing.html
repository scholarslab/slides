Makerspace Workshop: Introduction to Sewing

Tuesday, October 17
11:00am-12:30pm
Alderman Library, Room 423

Are you new to sewing? Would you like to learn the basics of operating a sewing machine? This workshop is for you. We will cover general sewing vocabulary, winding a bobbin and threading the machine, basic stitching, and basic understanding of needle and fabric type. Instructor: Paula Archey.
