GIS Workshop: Making Your First Map With ArcGIS

Wednesday, September 12
3:00 - 4:00pm
Alderman Library, Room 421

Here’s your chance to get started with geographic information systems software in a friendly, jargon-free environment. This workshop introduces the skills you need to make your own maps. Along the way you’ll get a taste of Earth’s most popular GIS software (ArcGIS) and a gentle introduction to cartography. You’ll leave with your own cartographic masterpieces and tips for learning more in your pursuit of mappiness at UVa. Instructors: Chris Gist, Drew Macqueen.
