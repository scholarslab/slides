GIS Workshop: Georeferencing a Map

Wednesday, February 4
1:00 -2:00pm
Alderman Library, Room 421

Would you like to see historic map overlaid on modern aerial photography? Do you need to extract features of a map for use in GIS? Georeferencing is the first step. We will show you how to take a scan of a paper map and align in it in ArcGIS. Instructor: Chris Gist.
