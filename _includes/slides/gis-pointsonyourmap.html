GIS Workshop: Points on Your Map – Street Addresses and More Spatial Things

Thursday, September 17
3:00 - 4:00pm
Alderman Library, Room 421

Do you have a list of street addresses crying out to be mapped? Have a list of zip codes or census tracts you wish to associate with other data? We’ll start with addresses and other things spatial and end with points on a map, ready for visualization and analysis. Instructor: Chris Gist.
