DH Fellows Panel & Luncheon

May 1, 2019
10:30
Alderman Room 421

Please join us for our Digital Humanities Graduate Student Fellows Panel and Luncheon! Panelists Kelli Shermeyer (English) and Sean Tennant (Art and Architectural History) will briefly present their projects and discuss how their digital methodologies have shaped their analyses. Fellows will also share the ways digital humanities has influenced their current and future research and teaching. Lunch will follow.
