GIS Day 2017 Celebration

November 15
2:00pm - 4:00pm
Scholars' Lab Common Room

The Scholars’ Lab celebrates GIS Day 2017! We’ll host lighting round talks – showcasing a variety of GIS projects from across Grounds and around Charlottesville – from 2:00 – 4:00 in the Scholars’ Lab Common Room. The reveal of our annual GIS Day cake will follow. All are welcome. Please join us for the festivities!
