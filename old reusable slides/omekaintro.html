Makerspace Workshop: Introduction to Omeka

Wednesday, September 23
1:00 - 2:30pm
Alderman Library, Room 423

Omeka was created to enable scholars, archives, libraries, museums, and independent researchers to build online exhibits of their work without having to know HTML or CSS. If you have a collection of digital resources that you want to exhibit on the web, Omeka could be a great tool to have in your toolkit. Instructor: Ronda Grizzle
